HNN Office Hours


 

Scheduling a Meeting


If you have an idea for using HNN in your research, but would like some advice on how to best get started, or for any individualized consultation on your project, please sign up for a consultation office hour with Dr. Jones. Please click here to view available consultation hours and include a short description of what you would like to discuss during your meeting. Please note, these meeting are not for technical support. For help with bug fixes or other technical issues please log your question on the Github Issues Page and someone from the team will get back to you shortly. You can also check out our Community Discussion Board for further information.
